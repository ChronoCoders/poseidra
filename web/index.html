The Problem

Blockchain is public.
Your strategy shouldn't be.

Every on-chain transaction you make is visible to everyone — your competitors, front-runners, and analytics firms. And existing privacy solutions either offer no compliance story, or bolt one on as an afterthought.

Exchanges block deposits from privacy protocols. Regulators scrutinize them. The tools that exist weren't built for institutions or serious traders.

👁
Your positions are fully visible
Anyone can see your wallet, your size, your strategy. Chain analytics firms build profiles on every active address.
⚖️
Privacy tools don't pass compliance
Exchanges block withdrawals from mixers. Tornado Cash proved that privacy without compliance is a legal liability.
🔗
You can't prove your funds are clean
Even if your funds are legitimate, you can't selectively disclose that fact without revealing everything.
How It Works

Deposit. Prove. Withdraw.

No one learns which deposit matches your withdrawal. But anyone can verify your funds are clean — without you revealing anything else.

01
Deposit
Send funds to the Poseidra contract with a cryptographic commitment. You hold the secret. Nobody else does.
02
Wait
As more deposits accumulate, your anonymity set grows. The larger the pool, the stronger your privacy.
03
Prove
Generate a zero-knowledge proof. It proves your deposit exists and your funds are clean — without linking withdrawal to deposit.
04
Withdraw
Receive funds at a new address via a relayer. On-chain: your proof is valid. Off-chain: your identity is private.

Why Poseidra

What no other protocol offers.

✓
Compliance is core, not a patch
Every other protocol added compliance after launch. Poseidra is designed compliance-first — association and exclusion set proofs are part of the base architecture.
✓
Prove innocence without revealing identity
Generate a ZK proof that your funds are not from a sanctioned source. Show it to any exchange or regulator. They learn nothing else about you.
✓
No trusted setup per circuit
PLONK with KZG uses a universal setup. Railgun requires a ceremony every time the circuit changes. Poseidra doesn't have that problem.

Formally specified and published
A full academic paper with a five-class adversary model, formal privacy proofs, and circuit specifications. Not marketing — mathematics.
